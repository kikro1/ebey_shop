Item Description

W3 External Steel Rear Retrieval Post Box with Trim for Gates and Fences

Providing the perfect solution if you would like your mail delivered to the front of your gate or fence and you wish to retrieve it without leaving your property. This gate mounted, rear access mailbox is made from high quality, galvanised steel and powder coated to withstand the great British weather. Complete with a viewing window which allows you to see when your mail has been delivered and a large door to make mail retrieval simple. We offer various sizes in this style, so please choose the model suited to your circumstances from the diagram pictured and see our other listings, comes with front and rear fitting trims.

This listing is for the W3 with trim which will give a nice finish to your raw edges if cutting a hole through your gate.

Features:

Correspondence suitable: A4, C4
Capacity: 9L
Front and rear fitting trims
Keys: 2 keys supplied
Lock: Cylinder lock
Warranty: 2 year

Dimensions:

W3 Model with Trims
Height: 400mm
Width: 295mm
Depth: 83mm
Front Trim Dimensions: 150mm x 335mm
Dimensions of letter slot: 33mm x 264mm

Your letterbox is pre-drilled and comes with a fixing kit included, however because of the variety in types of gates and fences, we cannot always provide the exact fixings required for your circumstances. Please ensure the fixing kit provided is suitable for your gate before fitting your letterbox.
